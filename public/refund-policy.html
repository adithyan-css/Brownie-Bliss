Refund Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with your sweet treats. Since our products are perishable and handmade to order, we have established the following refund policy.

1. Order Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled delivery time for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before delivery may not be eligible for a refund as the baking process may have already started.

2. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive an item that is damaged or does not match your order, please notify us immediately upon delivery. We will either:

  • Provide a replacement at the earliest possible time.
  • Issue a partial or full refund depending on the nature of the issue.

3. Quality Concerns

We take great pride in our quality. If you are unsatisfied with the quality of your dessert, please contact us within 12 hours of delivery with photos and details. We handle these cases on an individual basis to ensure a fair resolution.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are generally not provided in the following cases:

  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the customer.
  • Recipient not available at the time of delivery.
  • Minor variations in design/color as products are handcrafted.

5. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days via the original payment method or as store credit, as per your preference.
