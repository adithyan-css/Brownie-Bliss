Our Journey

From Kitchen to Your Doorstep

2022 — The Beginning
A Humble Kitchen Dream

Brownie Bliss was born in a home kitchen in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu. A passion for baking turned into a mission — to create desserts that taste just like home.

2023 — Growing Roots
Word Spread, Orders Soared

Happy customers shared their love, and Brownie Bliss grew organically through word-of-mouth. Custom birthday cakes and festive orders became our specialty.

2024 — Going Digital
Online Ordering & Wider Reach

We launched our online platform to bring homemade treats to more doorsteps. WhatsApp-based ordering made it easier than ever to enjoy our desserts.

2025 — Today & Beyond
Still Homemade. Always.

Despite our growth, every brownie is still made by hand, in small batches, with the same love and premium ingredients as day one. That will never change.

"Some desserts are eaten. Homemade desserts are remembered."

— The Brownie Bliss Philosophy
